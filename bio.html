Biography

Hristina Veljanoska was born in Macedonia and currently lives in Sweden where she finished her master degree in piano performance in May this year, at the Malmö Academy of Music (Lund University) under the tutelage of professor Hans Pålsson.

Since the age of 7, Hristina has won numerous national and international competitions, including the 1st prize at the American Protegé International Music Talent Competition after which she was invited and performed in Carnegie Hall, NYC in December 2017, 1st prize at the 15th International competition “Pietro Argento” held in Gioia del Cole in Italy, 3rd prize at the 3rd International competition “Piano Talents” held in Milan, Italy and 3rd prize at the 48th National competition for European union of Music Competitions for Youth (EMCY) held in Skopje, Macedonia.

She has taken part in music festivals and seminars such as the Classical Music Festival held in Eisenstadt, Austria where she has performed at the Estherhazy palace, Järna Festival Academy in Sweden and the Austrian International Piano Seminar held in Ebenfurth, Austria.

Hristina has participated in masterclasses with prominent pianists and pedagogues among which Ljudmila Romanova (Russia), Alan Chow (USA), Francisca Skoogh (Sweden), Nicholas Roth (USA), Peter Friis Johansson (Sweden), Adam Wibrowski (Poland) and William Wellborn (USA).

Hristina was especially honored to perform at the Baåstad Chamber Music Festival in Sweden in 2017, and by the invitation from the Spanish Ambassador in Macedonia to perform at a day dedicated to Spanish music at the Embassy.
