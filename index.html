classical pianist - performer - teacher

An accomplished young pianist, Hristina Veljanoska has captured audiences with sensitive musicality, colorful tones and emotional depth in her music since early beginnings. Throughout the years Hristina has won numerous international competitions including the 1st prize at the ‘Pietro Argento’ piano competition in Italy in 2012 and has performed at music festivals across Europe.

Hristina was especially honoured to perform at the Båstad Chamber Music Festival, one of the most renowned music festivals in Scandinavia, and at her debut performane in Carnegie Hall in New York City in 2017 as a first prize winner of the American Protégé International Music Talent Competition.
